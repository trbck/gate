# Charter — mentordrop

## Goal
Marketplace for 30-min calls with people who *did the thing* (exited, career-switched,
recovered) — not coaches. Buyer pays €49, mentor sets rate later, platform keeps 20%.

## Why this wins
AI gives advice; it can't give lived experience with reputation at stake. Curation
(application + reference call) is the moat. Incumbents (Clarity, Intro) sell polish,
not vetted lived experience.

## North star
**Metric:** € paid bookings/month · **Success:** 10 paid bookings in any 30-day window
by 2026-08-31 · **Now:** 0

## Kill criteria (pre-committed)
- Park if <3 paid bookings by 2026-08-15 despite 200+ landing visitors.
- Kill if 2026-08-31 passes with <10 total paid bookings.

## Non-goals (scope fence)
- NO custom scheduling/video/payment infra before 10 paid bookings (Calendly+Stripe only).
- NO mentor self-serve signup flow — manual vetting is the product.
- NO mobile app. NO AI matching. NO subscriptions in v1.

## Distribution & money (the non-code plan)
- First 10 users: 3 Reddit posts (r/cscareerquestions, r/startups) + 20 direct DMs.
- Pricing hypothesis: flat €49/30min.
- First euro charged when: L1 gate green (it already can be — charge from day one).
